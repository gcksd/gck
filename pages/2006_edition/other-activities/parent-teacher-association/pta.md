# Parent Teacher Association

The Principal is the President and the Treasurer of the association. A General
body meeting of the association is held every year and the office bearers of
the association are elected in this meeting. A member elected from among the
Parents is the Vice-President. The Secretary of the association is a teacher
elected from among the teachers.  
  
The Parent Teacher Association has extended its support to the manifold
activities of the college. It has always taken keen interest in the general
welfare of the students. The following are the various activities of the PTA
during the past three years.
