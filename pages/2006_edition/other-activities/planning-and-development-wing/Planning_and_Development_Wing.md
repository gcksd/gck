# Planning and Development Wing

The College has constituted a planning and development wing in the year 2004.
The main aim of the wing is to notify the details of the financial assistance
by the Governmental and non-Governmental organisations and put forward
necessary proposals for the development of the College. The committee attends
to the infrastructural and academic requirements of the institution keeping in
mind the needs for the coming five years. We expect that this attitude will
lead our institution to a prestigious centre of higher education in the near
future.
