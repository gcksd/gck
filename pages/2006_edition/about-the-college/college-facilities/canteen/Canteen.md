# College Canteen

There is a canteen in the college campus. The functioning of the canteen is
monitored by a committee constituted at the beginning of every academic year
by the College Council with the Principal as the Chairman. Quotations are
invited yearly, and based on the rates quoted sanction is given for running
the canteen. Food and beverages are supplied on subsidised rates.
